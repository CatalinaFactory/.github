Catalina Factory is an independent game studio founded in 2026 and based in Latvia. We are a
small team that develops original games and provides development services to other studios. We
work without a publisher and set our own schedule.

## Services

- **Original games.** In-house titles developed from prototype to release.
- **Gameplay systems and mechanics.** System design, implementation, and tuning.
- **3D art and environments.** Models, props, and environments with consistent art direction.
- **Animation and trailers.** Character animation and cinematic trailers.
- **Co-development.** Additional engineering and art capacity for projects in production.
- **Technical services.** Prototypes, tools, and pipelines for partner studios.
